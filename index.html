About

I came to software from an unusual place. I trained as an architect in Addis Ababa, worked on projects from Ethiopia to London, and co-founded a design company before moving to New York for a master's in Computer Science at NYU. That path shapes how I build. I care about structure, clarity and the people who will use what I make.

Today I build full-stack and cloud applications, work with data and develop AI tools. I am drawn to problems with real human stakes, in areas like immigration, education and community services. Away from the keyboard, you will find me reading, drawing, playing soccer or hiking.
